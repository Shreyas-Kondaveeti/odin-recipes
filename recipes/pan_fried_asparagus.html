Pan-Fried Asparagus

This is an extremely simple dish that can serve as a side to many meat and seafood dishes. The dish is Italian-style. Pan fried asparagus is delicious for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teapoon coarse salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet ove medium-high heat. Add olive oil, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir for 30 seconds with garlic.
  2. Add asparagus spears and cook until tender. Turn often until evenly cooked for 10 minutes.
