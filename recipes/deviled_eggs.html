Deviled Eggs Recipe

This is a recipe for simple, but delicious Deviled Eggs. These are perfect for parties or for fufilling snacks. Add garnishes to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 6 boiled eggs
  • Creamy mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Cut the boiled eggs in half. Remove yolks. Preserve whites.
  2. Mash yolks with a fork. Add remaining ingredients.
  3. Stuff or pipe filing into the egg halves.
