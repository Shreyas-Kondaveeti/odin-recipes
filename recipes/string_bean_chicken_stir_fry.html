String Bean Chicken Stir Fry

The image above is a recipe for String Bean Chicken Stir Fry. The recipe attempts to emulate authentic cuisine and will use authentic cooking methods and ingredients. THe dish is extremely delicious and will be wonderful for any one craving something spicy and fufilling. We are sure that this recipe will not disappoint, and will provide you with an authentic dish.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. boneless chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • 3 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 pound string beans
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • White pepper
  • 4 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic

Steps

  1. Add marinade ingredients into a bowl. Mix with hands and massage to make sure chicken absorbs all liquid.
  2. Prepare the sauce by mixing chicken stock, sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, and cornstarch.
  3. Preheat wok to medium high heat. Add 2 tablespoons of oil. Sear chicken till browned, then transfer to seperate bowl. Leave fat in wok.
  4. Add 2 tablesppons of oil to the wok. Add all string beans. Sear on one side for 1 minute. 30 seconds of stir-fry. Add 1/4 cup of water. Cover wok and allow steaming for 60 seconds.
  5. Add garlic an stir fry for 30 more seconds. Add sauce mixture and simmer.
  6. Add chicken back to the wok and stir-fry for 30 seconds. When matching the consistency desired, plate all food and serve with rice.
